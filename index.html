Social Run Club (SRC)

Who are we?

Exercising alone poses challenges, so as community members, our aim is to maintain both physical and mental well-being through a combination of running, social interaction, and shared activities such as walking, eating, and sitting together.

When and Where?

Every Saturday, we gather at approximately 3 pm in the city square and engage in a running session lasting about an hour. Upon returning, we often opt to have dinner at restaurants with which we have a partnership.

What are these restaurants?

When we go out for a run, we might not have cash or credit cards on hand. These restaurants focus on serving healthy food, and in such situations, they are open to allowing customers to settle the payment later.

Come join us to have fun!

Every Saturday at 3 pm, we come together. Feel free to join in and experience the enjoyment!
